Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my fav foods. Here is a recipe for delicously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan aover a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and creams in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted and cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the egde of the pan to the center every 20secs (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moise remove from the heat( it wil continue to cook on the plate until served
